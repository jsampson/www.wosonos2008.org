Schedule and Times for Events

Open Space Learning Workshop

Monday, July 21, and Tuesday, July 22, 2008—on Monday 8:30am to 8:00pm (08:30–20:00); on Tuesday 8:30am to 5:00pm (08:30–17:00)

Location: Fort Mason

Breakfast snacks, beverages, box lunches, and "the Open Space Idea Book" included

Separate fee, separate registration—for more information on content and pricing structure and to register (people of all levels of financial ability welcome), contact Lisa Heft at lisaheft@openingspace.net

Author Evening with Harrison Owen

Tuesday, July 22, 2008—7:00pm to 9:00pm (19:00–21:00)

Location: Golden Gate Room, Fort Mason

Light snacks and refreshments included

The 3rd Edition of Open Space Technology: A User's Guide will be launched and copies will be available for sale (US$)

Free event, open to the public—Please pre-register to help us in our food planning. If you register for the full conference, you will be indicating whether or not you plan to join us for the Author Evening. If you plan not to come to the conference but only for the Author Evening, please pre-register for the Author Evening with Jeff Aitken.

World Open Space on Open Space

Private event, for registered conference delegates only—please register for conference

Wednesday, July 23, 2008—6:00pm to 10:00pm (18:00–22:00)

Welcome gathering

Location: Golden Gate Room, Fort Mason

Snacks and refreshments included

Private event, for registered conference delegates only—separate ticket available for guests—please register for conference

Thursday, July 24, 2008—8:30am to 5:00pm (08:30–17:00)

WOSonOS—Day 1

Opening Circle and agenda co-creation, working sessions, Evening News

Location: Golden Gate Club, Presidio

Breakfast snacks; morning beverages, box lunches, and beverages included

Pack into your luggage some lovely artisan work, poetry, shawl or other item to sell to other OpenSpaceniks at Global Village Marketplace and Silent Auction—to raise money for your own expenses or for the Access Queen project; bring a little extra money if you like (US dollars please) to buy lovely things from your lovely colleagues.

Thursday evening, July 24, 2008—6:00pm to 10:00pm (18:00–22:00)

Dinner and celebration

Location: Golden Gate Room, Fort Mason

Dinner snacks (including surprises from our 'international suitcase cuisine') and beverages included

You may wish to pack into your luggage a lovely "hippie" costume to wear to the party plus a special food or beverage item from your country, if you like, to contribute to our buffet; pack also your dancing shoes.

Friday, July 25, 2008—8:30am to 5:00pm (08:30–17:00)

WOSonOS—Day 2

Morning News and announcements of late-breaking sessions, working sessions, Evening News

Location: Golden Gate Club, Presidio

Breakfast snacks; morning beverages, box lunch included; dinner on your own with friends

Saturday, July 26, 2008—8:30am to 3:00pm (08:30–15:00)

WOSonOS—Day 3

Morning News, idea generation session, invitations by future host teams, discussion and decision, announcement of WOSonOS 2009, lunch, Closing Circle comments and reflections.

Location: Golden Gate Club, Presidio

Breakfast snacks; morning beverages, box lunch included. Book of Proceedings (which will be sent to all registered delegates within several weeks after the event) also included.

Fabulous Facilitators

Location: Fort Mason

monthly breakfast 'pot-luck' meal and conversation hosted by Lisa Heft

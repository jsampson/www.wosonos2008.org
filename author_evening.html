Author Evening with Harrison Owen

Join Harrison Owen, the originator of Open Space Technology, as he launches the new, 3rd Edition of his book, Open Space Technology: A User's Guide—Berrett-Koehler Publishers, San Francisco 2008.

Enjoy an evening of stories, conversation and refreshments and be the first people in the world to celebrate and buy this book—the fundamental book on Open Space. The User's Guide explains the method, theory, values, and case studies of Open Space Technology.

Please pre-register for this free event so we can plan for refreshments.

For those of you who will be joining us for both the conference and the Author Evening, please register here.

For those of you coming only for the Author Evening, please let Jeff Aitken know you will be joining us.

Our thanks to Berrett-Koehler for generously underwriting a portion of this author evening.

When & Where:
Tuesday, July 22, 2008—7:00pm to 9:00pm (19:00–21:00)
Golden Gate Room, Fort Mason Center, Building A
